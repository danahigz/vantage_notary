Professional Digital Fingerprinting Services

At Vantage Notary, we offer state-of-the-art digital fingerprinting services for individuals and organizations requiring fingerprint-based background checks. Our advanced technology provides clear, accurate fingerprints that are directly submitted to the appropriate agencies.

What is Digital Fingerprinting?

Digital fingerprinting (also known as electronic fingerprinting or LiveScan) uses advanced scanning technology to capture fingerprints electronically rather than using traditional ink and paper methods. This technology provides clearer, more accurate fingerprints and allows for direct electronic submission to the appropriate agencies, resulting in faster processing times and more reliable results.

Common Uses for Digital Fingerprinting

Employment

Many employers require fingerprint-based background checks for positions involving security, financial responsibility, or working with vulnerable populations.

Professional Licensing

Many professions require fingerprinting for licensing, including teachers, healthcare workers, real estate agents, and security personnel.

Adoption

Fingerprinting is often required for domestic and international adoption processes to ensure child safety.

Immigration

Many immigration applications and processes require fingerprinting for background checks and identification verification.

Security Clearances

Government and military positions often require fingerprinting for security clearance background checks.

Legal Requirements

Various legal processes may require fingerprinting, including certain court proceedings and name changes.

Our Digital Fingerprinting Process

Our digital fingerprinting process is efficient and straightforward:

  1. Schedule an appointment - Contact us to arrange a convenient time for your fingerprinting session.
  2. Bring required documentation - Bring your government-issued photo ID and any specific forms or information required by the requesting agency.
  3. Digital capture - We use state-of-the-art equipment to capture your fingerprints electronically.
  4. Quality check - We verify the quality of the fingerprint scans to ensure they meet agency standards.
  5. Electronic submission - We submit your fingerprints directly to the appropriate agency or provide you with the necessary files.
  6. Confirmation - You receive confirmation of the submission for your records.
