About Vantage Notary

Professional, reliable notary services with a personal touch.

Our Story

Vantage Notary was founded with a simple mission: to provide professional, reliable notary services with a focus on convenience and customer satisfaction. We recognized that traditional notary services often required clients to travel to a fixed location during limited business hours, which could be inconvenient for many people.

To address this challenge, we assembled a team of professional notaries who could offer flexible scheduling and mobile services throughout the Bluffton and Fort Wayne areas. Our approach gives clients the advantage of having their documents notarized when and where it's most convenient for them.

Our Approach

Professionalism

Convenience

Reliability
