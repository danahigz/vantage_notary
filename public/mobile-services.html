Professional Mobile Notary Services

At Vantage Notary, we understand that visiting a notary office isn't always convenient. That's why we offer comprehensive mobile notary services throughout the Bluffton and Fort Wayne areas. Our professional notaries will come to your location at a time that works for you, bringing all the necessary equipment to provide seamless notarization services.

Why Choose Our Mobile Notary Service?

Convenience

Save time and avoid travel by having a notary come to your preferred location, whether it's your home, office, hospital, care facility, or another location.

Flexible Scheduling

We offer appointments during evenings and weekends to accommodate your busy schedule when traditional notary offices are closed.

Multiple Notaries

With multiple notaries on staff, we can accommodate urgent requests and ensure availability when you need us most.

Extensive Service Area

We serve the entire Bluffton and Fort Wayne areas, providing mobile notary services within a 30-mile radius.

Mobile Services We Offer

Our mobile notaries can provide all of our standard services at your location:

Standard Notarizations

We can notarize a wide range of documents, including powers of attorney, affidavits, wills, trusts, and more.

Loan Signings

Our certified loan signing agents can come to your home or office to facilitate real estate closings and other loan document signings.

Digital Fingerprinting

We offer mobile digital fingerprinting services for background checks, licensing requirements, and other needs.

Multiple Signers

We can accommodate documents requiring multiple signers at the same location, making it convenient for everyone involved.

Scheduling a Mobile Notary

Scheduling our mobile notary service is simple:

  1. Contact us by phone, email, or through our website contact form
  2. Provide details about your document(s) and the type of notarization needed
  3. Specify your preferred location, date, and time
  4. We'll confirm availability and schedule your appointment
  5. Our notary will arrive at the scheduled time with all necessary equipment

We recommend scheduling at least 24 hours in advance when possible, though we can often accommodate same-day requests.

Pricing

Our mobile notary services are priced as follows:

  • Standard notarization fee: $15 per signature/seal
  • Travel fee: $25 within 20 miles of our location
  • Additional mileage fee: $1 per mile beyond 20 miles
  • Loan signing packages: Starting at $125 plus travel fee
  • Digital fingerprinting: $50 per person plus travel fee
