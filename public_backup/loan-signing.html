Professional Loan Signing Agent Services

At Vantage Notary, our loan signing agents are specially trained and certified to handle real estate transactions and loan document signings. We understand the importance of these transactions and provide professional, efficient service to ensure a smooth closing process.

What is a Loan Signing Agent?

A loan signing agent is a notary public who specializes in facilitating mortgage loan closings. Our loan signing agents have additional training and expertise in handling and explaining loan documents. They are certified by the National Notary Association and are familiar with all types of loan documents, ensuring a smooth and efficient closing process.

Types of Loan Signings We Handle

Purchase Loans

For new home purchases, including conventional, FHA, VA, and USDA loans.

Refinance Loans

For homeowners refinancing their existing mortgage to secure better terms or rates.

Home Equity Loans

For homeowners accessing the equity in their homes through loans or lines of credit.

Commercial Loans

For business property purchases, refinances, and commercial real estate transactions.

The Loan Signing Process

Our loan signing process is designed to be thorough yet efficient:

  1. Appointment Scheduling - We coordinate with all parties to arrange a convenient time and location for the signing.
  2. Document Preparation - We review the loan package in advance to ensure a smooth signing process.
  3. Document Explanation - Our signing agents explain each document in clear, understandable terms.
  4. Proper Execution - We ensure all documents are signed correctly and in the proper order.
  5. Notarization - We notarize all documents requiring notarization according to state law.
  6. Document Return - We ensure the completed documents are returned promptly to the appropriate parties.
